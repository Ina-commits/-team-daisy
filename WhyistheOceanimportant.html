Why Is The Ocean Important?

Provides The Air We Breathe

The ocean provides over 50% of the world's oxygen and stores 50 times more carbon dioxide than our atmosphere.

Regulates The Global Climate

Covering 70% of the Earth's surface, the ocean transports heat from the equator to the poles, regulating our climate and weather patterns.

Recreation

From fishing to boating to kayaking and dolphin watching, the ocean provides us with so many unique activities.

Medicine

Many medical products come from the ocean, including ingredients that help fight cancer, arthritis, Alzheimer's disease and heart disease.

Transportation

76% percent of all U.S. trade involve some form of marine transportation.

Food

The ocean provides much more than just seafood. ingredients from the sea are found in suprising foods such as peanut butter and soymilk.
